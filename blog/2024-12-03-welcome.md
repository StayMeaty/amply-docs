Welcome to the official Amply documentation site!

We're excited to launch this comprehensive resource for donors, non-profit organizations, and developers who want to learn more about the Amply platform.

<!-- truncate -->

## What You'll Find Here

### For Donors
Learn how to make your first donation, track the impact of your giving, and understand the tax benefits available to you.

### For Organizations
Discover how to join Amply, meet our requirements, and report your impact to supporters.

### For Developers
Explore our API documentation and learn how to integrate with the Amply platform.

## Contributing

This documentation is open source. If you find any issues or have suggestions for improvement, please visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/StayMeaty/amply-docs) to contribute.

## Stay Updated

We'll be publishing regular updates about new features, best practices, and success stories from our community. Subscribe to our RSS feed to stay informed.

Thank you for being part of the Amply community!
