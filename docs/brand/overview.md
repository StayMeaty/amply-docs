# Brand Overview

Guidelines for presenting Amply consistently across all touchpoints.

## Core Principle

Amply's visual identity reflects its mission: **clarity through transparency**. Every design choice serves readability, accessibility, and trust.

## Brand Elements

### Typography

**Lexend** is our primary typeface—chosen for its accessibility benefits and clean aesthetic.

- Headlines: Thin/ExtraLight weights for a light, modern feel
- Body: Regular weight for comfortable reading
- Brand name: Black weight as our wordmark

→ [Typography Guide](./typography.md)

### The Wordmark

**Amply** in Lexend Black (900) serves as our text logo. Simple, bold, recognizable.

## Design Principles

1. **Accessible by default** — Every choice considers all users
2. **Clarity over decoration** — Functional, not ornamental
3. **Consistent, not rigid** — Guidelines enable, not constrain
4. **Trust through transparency** — Visual honesty matches operational honesty
