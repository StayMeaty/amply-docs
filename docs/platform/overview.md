# Platform Overview

Amply is designed to make charitable giving simple, transparent, and impactful.

## Our Mission

Amply connects generous donors with effective non-profit organizations, creating a transparent ecosystem where every contribution makes a measurable difference.

## How It Works

### For Donors

1. **Discover**: Browse verified non-profit organizations
2. **Give**: Make secure one-time or recurring donations
3. **Track**: Monitor your impact through detailed reporting
4. **Connect**: Engage with the causes you support

### For Organizations

1. **Apply**: Submit your organization for verification
2. **Profile**: Create a compelling presence on Amply
3. **Receive**: Accept donations with low fees
4. **Report**: Share impact with your supporters

## Key Features

- **Verified Organizations**: Every organization is vetted for legitimacy
- **Transparent Fees**: Clear, low-cost donation processing
- **Impact Tracking**: Real metrics on donation outcomes
- **Tax Documentation**: Automatic receipt generation
- **Multi-Language Support**: Available in English and German
- **Secure Payments**: Bank-level security for all transactions

## Platform Principles

### Transparency

We believe donors deserve to know exactly where their money goes and what impact it creates.

### Efficiency

Low overhead means more of every donation reaches those in need.

### Trust

Rigorous vetting and ongoing monitoring ensure quality organizations.

### Accessibility

Simple, intuitive design makes giving easy for everyone.
