# Track Your Impact

Amply provides comprehensive tools to help you understand the real-world impact of your donations.

## Your Impact Dashboard

Every donor has access to a personalized dashboard showing:

- **Total Contributions**: Lifetime giving across all organizations
- **Active Subscriptions**: Recurring donations you've set up
- **Impact Metrics**: Tangible outcomes from your support
- **Organizations Supported**: Complete list of causes you've helped

## Understanding Impact Metrics

We work with organizations to provide meaningful impact data:

### Quantitative Metrics

- Number of beneficiaries reached
- Resources distributed
- Projects funded
- Geographic reach

### Qualitative Updates

- Success stories from beneficiaries
- Progress reports from organizations
- Photo and video updates
- Milestone achievements

## Impact Reports

Receive regular reports showing:

- How your donations were allocated
- Specific outcomes achieved
- Comparison to goals
- Future plans and needs

## Sharing Your Impact

Inspire others by sharing your giving journey:

- Generate shareable impact summaries
- Display badges on social media
- Create giving circles with friends and family
