# How to Donate

Making a donation through Amply is simple and secure. This guide walks you through the process step by step.

## Creating an Account

Before making your first donation, you'll need to create an Amply account:

1. Visit the Amply website
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile information

## Finding Causes to Support

Amply connects you with verified non-profit organizations. You can discover causes through:

- **Browse by Category**: Explore organizations by focus area (education, environment, health, etc.)
- **Search**: Look for specific organizations or causes
- **Featured Campaigns**: Discover highlighted initiatives and urgent needs
- **Recommendations**: Get personalized suggestions based on your interests

## Making a Donation

Once you've found a cause you want to support:

1. Select the organization or campaign
2. Choose your donation amount
3. Select donation frequency (one-time or recurring)
4. Enter payment information
5. Review and confirm your donation

## Payment Methods

Amply accepts various payment methods:

- Credit/Debit cards
- Bank transfers
- Digital wallets

## Donation Confirmation

After your donation is processed, you'll receive:

- An email confirmation with transaction details
- A tax-deductible receipt (where applicable)
- Updates on how your donation is being used
