# Tax Benefits

Donations made through Amply to eligible organizations may qualify for tax deductions. This guide explains how to maximize your tax benefits.

## Tax-Deductible Donations

Most donations made through Amply qualify as tax-deductible contributions. Each organization's tax status is clearly displayed on their profile.

## Obtaining Tax Receipts

Amply automatically provides tax receipts for your donations:

1. Receipts are emailed immediately after each donation
2. Annual summary receipts are available in January
3. Access all receipts from your account dashboard

## What Information is Included

Your tax receipt includes:

- Organization's legal name and tax ID
- Date and amount of donation
- Confirmation that no goods or services were received
- Your donor information

## Record Keeping

We recommend:

- Save all donation receipts
- Download your annual giving summary
- Keep records for at least 7 years
- Consult with a tax professional for specific advice

## International Donors

Tax benefits vary by country. Please consult local tax regulations and a qualified tax advisor for guidance specific to your jurisdiction.

## Disclaimer

This information is provided for general guidance only and does not constitute tax advice. Please consult a qualified tax professional for advice specific to your situation.
