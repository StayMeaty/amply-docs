# Fundraising Guidelines
*Rules for Fundraising on Amply*

These guidelines ensure fundraising on Amply is trustworthy, transparent, and effective. All fundraisers must follow these rules.

## Core Principles

### Honesty

All campaign content must be truthful:
- Accurate descriptions of organizations
- Honest personal stories
- No exaggerated or false claims
- No misleading use of data or images

### Transparency

Be open with donors:
- Funds go directly to organizations
- No personal benefit from donations
- Clear about your relationship to the cause
- Honest about what donations will achieve

### Respect

Treat everyone with respect:
- Donors, organizations, and other fundraisers
- Don't pressure or guilt-trip
- Respect privacy
- Accept decisions gracefully

## Campaign Requirements

### Content Standards

**Allowed:**
- Personal stories and connections
- Organization information from official sources
- Accurate impact data and outcomes
- Photos you have rights to use
- Links to verified information

**Not Allowed:**
- False or misleading claims
- Graphic violence or disturbing imagery
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Political campaign content
- Commercial promotion
- Content that infringes others' rights

### Organization Selection

You may only fundraise for:
- Amply-verified organizations
- Organizations that accept fundraisers
- Causes you can honestly represent

You may not:
- Fundraise for yourself or personal gain
- Create campaigns for non-verified entities
- Misrepresent your relationship to organizations

### Goal Setting

Set realistic goals:
- Based on your actual reach
- Achievable within your timeframe
- Honest about what the amount will accomplish

Avoid:
- Inflated goals for attention
- Goals that imply specific outcomes you can't guarantee
- Promises about organizational actions you can't control

## Fundraiser Conduct

### Verification Requirements

All fundraisers must:
- Complete identity verification
- Provide accurate personal information
- Update information if it changes
- Respond to verification requests

### Campaign Management

You must:
- Monitor your campaign
- Respond to questions promptly
- Provide updates to donors
- Report any issues to Amply

You may not:
- Abandon active campaigns
- Ignore donor concerns
- Make changes that mislead existing donors
- Transfer campaign ownership without approval

### Communication

**With Donors:**
- Thank them appropriately
- Provide updates as promised
- Be honest about outcomes
- Protect their privacy

**About Organizations:**
- Use accurate information
- Don't speak on behalf of organizations
- Direct detailed questions to organizations
- Share official updates, not speculation

## Prohibited Activities

### Fraud and Deception

Absolutely prohibited:
- Creating campaigns for fake or unverified causes
- Misrepresenting how funds will be used
- Using someone else's identity or story
- Creating fake urgency or emergencies
- Manipulating donors through false information

### Financial Misconduct

You may not:
- Suggest funds will go to you
- Imply personal access to funds
- Request donations outside Amply
- Offer rewards or incentives
- Create multiple accounts to circumvent limits

### Platform Abuse

Prohibited:
- Automated campaign creation
- Spam or excessive messaging
- Harassment of donors or others
- Circumventing verification
- Exploiting platform vulnerabilities

## Sensitive Topics

### Emergency and Disaster Campaigns

For urgent crisis fundraising:
- Only for verified organizations responding
- Must accurately represent the situation
- No exploitation of tragedy
- Update as situations evolve

### Medical Campaigns

If fundraising for health causes:
- Verify organization actually provides stated services
- Don't make promises about individual outcomes
- Respect privacy of any individuals mentioned
- Be accurate about medical information

### Memorial Campaigns

For honoring someone:
- Have appropriate connection or permission
- Respect the wishes of family
- Accurately represent the person
- Don't exploit grief

## Enforcement

### Review Process

Campaigns are reviewed for:
- Accuracy of claims
- Appropriate content
- Guideline compliance
- Verification status

Most campaigns approved within 24 hours.

### Violations

If guidelines are violated:

**Minor violations:**
- Request to modify campaign
- Temporary pause
- Warning issued

**Serious violations:**
- Campaign removal
- Account suspension
- Permanent ban
- Report to authorities if legally required

### Appeals

If you disagree with enforcement:
- Contact Amply support
- Provide explanation
- Submit additional information
- Await review decision

## Reporting Concerns

If you see something wrong:

- Use report function on campaigns
- Email safety@amply-impact.org
- Reports are confidential
- Reviewed within 48 hours

- Material changes require agreement
