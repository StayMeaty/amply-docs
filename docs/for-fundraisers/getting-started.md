# Getting Started
*Launch Your First Fundraising Campaign*

This guide walks you through creating and running a fundraising campaign on Amply.

## Before You Begin

### Understand the Model

Key things to know:
- You collect donations on behalf of organizations
- Funds go directly to the organization, not to you
- You need to complete identity verification
- All campaigns are reviewed before going live

### Choose Your Organization

Browse Amply's verified organizations:
- Search by name
- Filter by SDG category
- Review transparency scores
- Check what they're working on

You can only fundraise for verified organizations.

## Creating Your Campaign

### Step 1: Identity Verification

One-time verification protects everyone:

**What's Required:**
- Government-issued ID
- Current address
- Brief background check

**Why It Matters:**
- Protects donors from fraud
- Protects organizations from misrepresentation
- Protects you from false associations
- Maintains platform integrity

Verification typically takes 1-2 business days.

### Step 2: Select Organization

Choose who you're fundraising for:

**Direct Selection:**
- Search by name
- View profile and mission
- See current campaigns
- Check organization preferences (some may not accept fundraisers)

**SDG Category:**
- Choose a cause area
- Let donors choose specific organizations
- Or allocate to multiple organizations

### Step 3: Campaign Details

Define your campaign:

**Basic Information:**
- Campaign title (clear and compelling)
- Goal amount
- Duration (open-ended or deadline)
- Category (birthday, memorial, challenge, etc.)

**Your Story:**
- Why this cause matters to you
- Personal connection
- What you hope to achieve
- Call to action

**Media:**
- Cover photo
- Additional images
- Video (optional but powerful)

### Step 4: Customize Your Page

Make it yours:

- Campaign URL (shareable link)
- Color theme
- Donation suggested amounts
- Thank-you message

### Step 5: Review and Submit

Before going live:
- Preview your page
- Check all information
- Review guidelines compliance
- Submit for approval

### Step 6: Approval

Amply reviews campaigns for:
- Accurate organization selection
- Appropriate content
- No misleading claims
- Guidelines compliance

Most campaigns approved within 24 hours.

## Launching Your Campaign

### Go Live

Once approved:
- Campaign page is public
- Shareable link active
- Ready to accept donations

### Share Immediately

First momentum matters:

**Social Media:**
- Post on your profiles
- Share in relevant groups
- Tag friends who might care
- Use organization's hashtags

**Personal Outreach:**
- Email close contacts first
- Personal messages convert better
- Ask for shares, not just donations

**Your Network:**
- Family and friends first
- Colleagues and communities
- People with connection to the cause

### Set the Tone

Lead by example:
- Consider making the first donation
- Share your personal connection
- Show enthusiasm and authenticity

## During Your Campaign

### Keep Momentum

**Regular Updates:**
- Post progress updates
- Share donor milestones
- Thank donors publicly
- Add new content

**Engage Donors:**
- Respond to comments
- Share on social media
- Send personal thanks
- Report on organization updates

### Track Progress

Your dashboard shows:
- Total raised
- Number of donors
- Goal progress
- Recent donations
- Sharing statistics

### Adjust as Needed

If things slow down:
- Post new content
- Reach new audiences
- Create urgency near deadline
- Ask for shares, not just donations

## After Your Campaign

### Celebrate and Thank

Whether you hit your goal or not:
- Thank all donors
- Share final total
- Celebrate the impact
- Acknowledge supporters

### Report Back

Keep donors informed:
- Share organization updates
- Report on how funds are used
- Show impact when available
- Maintain relationships

### Consider Next Steps

After a successful campaign:
- Run another campaign
- Start a Moral Circle
- Become a recurring donor
- Spread the word about Amply

## Campaign Tips

### What Works

- **Personal stories**: Share why you care
- **Specific goals**: "Fund 10 school supplies kits"
- **Clear asks**: Tell people exactly what to do
- **Visual content**: Photos and videos increase giving
- **Regular updates**: Keep momentum going
- **Personal outreach**: Individual asks outperform broadcasts

### What Doesn't

- Vague or generic appeals
- Set-and-forget campaigns
- Only broadcast sharing
- Unrealistic goals
- Unclear organization connection

### Best Practices

1. **Start strong**: Early momentum attracts more
2. **Be authentic**: Your story matters
3. **Make it easy**: Clear links, simple asks
4. **Thank immediately**: Donors feel appreciated
5. **Update regularly**: Show progress
6. **Close well**: Final push, then gratitude

## Common Questions

### What if I don't reach my goal?

- All donations still go to the organization
- Partial fundraising is still impactful
- You can extend deadlines
- Celebrate what you raised

### Can I fundraise for multiple organizations?

- Each campaign supports one organization
- You can run multiple campaigns
- Or use SDG-based giving

### What if I need to cancel?

- Contact Amply support
- Donations already made still go to organization
- Campaign removed from public view
- Document reason for records

### Do I get reports on how money was used?

- Organization provides updates
- You can share with donors
- Impact timing depends on project
- Stay connected with organization
