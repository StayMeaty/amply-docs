# Welcome to Amply

Welcome to the official Amply documentation. Amply is a platform dedicated to connecting donors with impactful non-profit organizations, making charitable giving transparent, efficient, and rewarding.

## What is Amply?

Amply is a non-profit platform that helps individuals and organizations make meaningful contributions to causes they care about. We believe in the power of collective action and transparent giving.
