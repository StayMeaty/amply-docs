# For Donors
*Give Confidently, Track Every Cent*

Amply helps individual donors find effective causes, make donations, and verify exactly where their money goes.

:::note For Businesses
If you're representing a business interested in corporate giving or customer donation programs, see [For Businesses](../for-businesses/overview.md).
:::

## What Amply Offers Donors

### Find Effective Causes

Discover where your giving can have the most impact:

- **SDG-organized causes**: Browse by the 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals
- **Verified organizations**: Only legitimate, verified nonprofits
- **Effectiveness indicators**: See how organizations measure and report impact
- **Transparency scores**: Compare openness across organizations

→ [Finding Causes](./finding-causes.md)

### Give With Confidence

Donate knowing your money goes where intended:

- **Zero platform fees**: Amply takes nothing—only payment processing costs
- **Direct transfers**: Your donation goes straight to the organization
- **Verified recipients**: All organizations verified before joining Amply
- **Fraud protection**: Comprehensive verification and monitoring

→ [Pricing and fees](../transparency/pricing.md)

### Track Your Impact

Follow your donation from gift to outcome:

- **Real-time ledger**: See your donation the moment it's processed
- **Organization updates**: Follow how your money is used
- **Project tracking**: Connect donations to specific programs
- **Impact reports**: See what your giving achieved

→ [Tracking Impact](./tracking-impact.md)

### Verify Everything

Don't just trust—verify:

- **Public ledger**: Every transaction visible and auditable
- **Cryptographic proof**: Records can't be altered after recording
- **Stripe anchoring**: Donations link to independent payment records
- **Independent verification**: Check the math yourself

→ [How verification works](../transparency/verification.md)

## Why Give Through Amply

### Transparency You Can Verify

Most donation platforms ask you to trust them. Amply proves it:

- Every donation recorded on public ledger
- Organizations' spending visible (with privacy for individuals)
- Hash chains ensure records can't be altered after recording
- Stripe payment references provide independent verification
- Anyone can audit the data

### Focus on Effectiveness

Amply attracts organizations that care about impact:

- Effectiveness metrics help you compare
- SDG alignment shows what problems organizations address
- Impact reporting shows results, not just activities
- GiveWell-inspired evaluation principles

### No Hidden Costs

Amply takes zero platform fees. Your donation arrives as intended:

- No percentage taken by Amply
- Only unavoidable payment processing fees
- Option to cover fees yourself so organizations receive full amount
- Fee-free options available (bank transfers)

### Privacy When You Want It

Control your visibility:

- Donate publicly to inspire others
- Donate privately for personal causes
- Anonymous giving available
- Your data protected, your generosity visible (if you choose)

## How Giving Works

### 1. Find a Cause

Browse organizations by:
- SDG category (climate, health, education, etc.)
- Location and reach
- Effectiveness indicators
- Transparency scores

Or donate to a specific organization you already know.

### 2. Make Your Donation

Choose your giving:
- One-time or recurring
- Amount (suggested or custom)
- Fee coverage preference
- Visibility preference

### 3. Receive Confirmation

Immediately:
- Transaction confirmation email
- Receipt for your records
- Link to verify on public ledger
- Tax documentation (where applicable)

### 4. Track Results

Over time:
- See your donation in organization's ledger
- Receive updates on fund usage
- View impact reports
- Follow the story of your giving

## Types of Giving

### One-Time Donations

Give when you want to causes you care about. Every gift, large or small, is tracked with the same transparency.

### Recurring Giving

Set up monthly, quarterly, or annual donations:
- Automatic processing
- Pause or modify anytime
- Combined impact reporting
- Predictable support for organizations

### Cause-Area Giving

Not sure which organization? Give to an SDG category:
- Select one or more SDGs
- Amply allocates to verified, effective organizations
- See where your funds go
- Adjust allocations anytime

### Campaign Donations

Support specific campaigns:
- Disaster relief
- Special projects
- Matching opportunities
- Time-limited initiatives

### Giving Circles

Join others in collective giving:
- Moral Circles (influencer-led communities)
- Group campaigns
- Shared decision-making
- Collective impact tracking

→ [For Fundraisers](../for-fundraisers/overview.md)
