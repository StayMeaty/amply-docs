# Tracking Impact
*Follow Your Donation From Gift to Outcome*

Amply provides unprecedented visibility into how your donations are used. This document explains how to track your giving's journey.

## The Donation Journey

### Stage 1: Transaction Complete

The moment you donate:
- Transaction recorded on Amply ledger
- Confirmation email sent
- Visible on your donor dashboard
- Appears in organization's incoming funds

### Stage 2: Funds Received

Within payment processing time (usually 1-3 days):
- Funds clear to organization
- Payout processed (per organization's schedule)
- Funds available for use

### Stage 3: Allocation

Organization allocates your donation:
- Assigned to fund or project (if designated)
- Added to general operations (if unrestricted)
- Tracked on organization's ledger

### Stage 4: Expenditure

Organization uses funds:
- Spending visible on their public ledger
- Categorized by type and purpose
- Connected to programs and projects

### Stage 5: Impact

Organization reports outcomes:
- Project updates
- Impact metrics
- Beneficiary stories
- Results data

**Impact Feed**
- Updates from organizations you've supported

**Tracking Links**
- Direct links to your transactions on the ledger

### Public Ledger

Verify your donation independently:

- Transaction appears in organization's incoming funds

### Organization Pages

See how organizations use all donations:

- Total incoming funds

## Types of Tracking

### General Donations

Unrestricted gifts:
- Tracked as organizational income
- Visible in spending categories
- Cannot be followed to specific use
- Most flexibility for organization

### Designated Donations

Gifts to specific funds or projects:
- Tracked to designated purpose
- Spending visible within that fund
- Project-level updates
- More specific attribution

### Campaign Donations

Gifts to fundraising campaigns:
- Campaign progress visible
- Goal tracking
- Updates from campaign organizers
- Final results reported

## Impact Reporting

### What Organizations Report

Depending on the organization:

**Activity Reports**
- What they did
- Where they worked
- Who they reached
- Resources deployed

**Outcome Reports**
- What changed
- Measured results
- Beneficiary feedback

**Impact Metrics**

- Lives affected

### Report Frequency

Varies by organization:

### Accessing Reports

Through Amply:
- Organization profile pages
- Embedded reports
- Downloadable PDFs
- Data exports

## Verifying Your Impact

### Transaction Verification

Confirm your donation was recorded:

1. Find transaction in your history
2. Click verification link
3. View on public ledger
4. Compare details to your receipt

### Ledger Integrity

Verify the ledger itself:

- Hash chain ensures no post-recording tampering
- Stripe payment IDs provide independent verification
- Public checkpoints confirm consistency over time
- Anyone can verify mathematically

→ [How to verify](../transparency/verification.md)

### Organization Verification

Confirm organization legitimacy:

- Verification badge displayed
- Registration documents on file
- Ongoing monitoring by Amply
- Any concerns flagged publicly

→ [Trust and Safety](../trust-and-safety/overview.md)

## Managing Expectations

### What Amply Can Show

- Financial transactions
- Organizational spending categories
- Fund allocations
- Reported outcomes

### What Organizations Determine

- Level of project detail shared
- Outcome measurement approach
- Update frequency
- Narrative context

### Realities of Impact Measurement

Honest impact tracking has limits:
- Causation is hard to prove
- Long-term effects take time
- Some outcomes are hard to measure
- Context affects results

Amply provides transparency. Organizations provide interpretation.
