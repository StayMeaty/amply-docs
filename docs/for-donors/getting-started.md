# Getting Started
*Make Your First Donation*

This guide walks you through making your first donation on Amply.

## Quick Start

### Donate Without an Account

You can give immediately:

1. **Find an organization** or campaign
2. **Enter donation amount**
3. **Provide payment details**
4. **Complete donation**

That's it. You'll receive:
- Email confirmation
- Tax receipt
- Link to verify on public ledger

No account required for one-time giving.

### Create an Account (Optional)

An account unlocks additional features:

- **Giving history**: All donations in one place
- **Recurring giving**: Set up automatic donations
- **Saved payment methods**: Faster checkout
- **Impact tracking**: Consolidated updates
- **Tax documentation**: Annual summaries
- **Preferences**: Visibility settings, communication options

## Making a Donation

### Step 1: Select Amount

Choose your gift:

- **Preset amounts**: Common giving levels
- **Custom amount**: Any amount you choose
- **Recurring**: Monthly, quarterly, or annual

### Step 2: Choose Fee Handling

Payment processing costs money (approximately 2-3%). You decide:

- **Cover fees** (recommended): Organization receives full amount
- **Deduct fees**: Fees taken from donation

Example at €100:
- Cover fees: You pay ~€103, organization receives €100
- Deduct fees: You pay €100, organization receives ~€97

→ [Pricing and fees](../transparency/pricing.md)

### Step 3: Set Visibility

Control how your donation appears:

| Option | What's Visible |
|--------|----------------|
| Public | Name and amount on ledger |
| Amount only | Amount visible, name hidden |
| Anonymous | Neither name nor amount publicly visible |

Note: Even anonymous donations are recorded on the ledger and can be verified by you.

### Step 4: Enter Payment

Amply accepts:

- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank transfers (SEPA, ACH)
- Digital wallets (where available)

Bank transfers often have lower or no fees.

### Step 5: Complete Donation

Review and confirm:

- Donation amount
- Fee handling
- Recipient organization
- Visibility setting

Submit to complete. Your donation is processed immediately.

## After Your Donation

### Immediate Confirmation

You receive:

- **Email receipt**: Confirmation with details
- **Transaction link**: Direct link to ledger entry
- **Tax documentation**: PDF receipt for records

### On the Ledger

Your donation is visible:

- Real-time posting
- Transaction hash for verification
- Organization's incoming funds updated
- Your giving history updated (if logged in)

### From the Organization

Expect:

- Thank-you message (organization-specific)
- Updates on fund usage
- Impact reports (when available)

## Recurring Giving

## Common Questions

### Is my donation tax-deductible?

Donations to qualified charitable organizations may be tax-deductible. Amply provides documentation; consult your tax advisor for specific treatment.

→ [Tax Benefits](./tax-benefits.md)

### What if I made a mistake?

Contact support within 24 hours. We can cancel or modify before funds are disbursed.

### Can I give to multiple organizations at once?

Yes. Give to multiple organizations in separate transactions, or give to an SDG category for automatic allocation.

### How do I know my money arrived?

Check the public ledger. Your donation appears on both your history and the organization's incoming funds.
