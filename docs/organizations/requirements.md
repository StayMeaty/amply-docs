# Requirements and Eligibility

This page outlines the requirements for organizations to join Amply.

## Eligibility Criteria

To join Amply, organizations must:

### Legal Requirements

- Be a registered non-profit organization
- Have valid tax-exempt status
- Maintain good standing with regulatory bodies
- Operate in compliance with local laws

### Operational Requirements

- Have been operational for at least 1 year
- Demonstrate active programming
- Maintain proper financial records
- Have a functioning governing board

### Mission Alignment

Organizations should:

- Have a clear charitable mission
- Focus on positive social impact
- Operate with transparency and accountability
- Commit to ethical practices

## Required Documentation

Prepare the following for your application:

- Certificate of incorporation
- Tax-exempt status documentation
- Most recent annual report
- Financial statements (last 2 years)
- Board member list
- Organization bylaws

## Ongoing Requirements

Once accepted, organizations must:

- Submit annual compliance updates
- Provide impact reports quarterly
- Maintain accurate profile information
- Respond to donor inquiries promptly
- Adhere to Amply's terms of service

## Exclusions

Amply does not accept organizations that:

- Promote discrimination
- Engage in political campaigning
- Operate primarily for private benefit
- Have unresolved legal issues

## Questions?

Contact our organization team if you're unsure about eligibility.
