# Impact Reporting

Transparent reporting builds donor trust and demonstrates your organization's effectiveness.

## Reporting Requirements

Organizations on Amply commit to regular impact reporting:

### Quarterly Updates

- Brief progress summaries
- Key metrics and milestones
- Photos or media (optional but encouraged)
- Upcoming plans

### Annual Reports

- Comprehensive impact overview
- Financial summary
- Beneficiary stories
- Year-over-year comparisons

## What to Report

### Quantitative Data

- Number of beneficiaries served
- Geographic reach
- Resources distributed
- Programs delivered
- Volunteers engaged

### Qualitative Information

- Success stories
- Challenges overcome
- Lessons learned
- Future goals

### Financial Transparency

- How donations were allocated
- Administrative costs
- Program spending
- Reserve funds

## Reporting Tools

Amply provides tools to simplify reporting:

- Dashboard analytics
- Report templates
- Media upload
- Donor communication tools

## Best Practices

- Be specific and honest
- Use data to tell your story
- Include beneficiary voices (with consent)
- Acknowledge challenges as well as successes
- Thank donors for their support

## Donor Notifications

When you submit reports, donors receive:

- Email notifications
- Dashboard updates
- Optional push notifications
