# Getting Started for Organizations

Welcome to Amply! This guide helps non-profit organizations get started on our platform.

## Why Join Amply?

Amply provides non-profits with:

- **Expanded Reach**: Connect with engaged donors looking to make a difference
- **Transparent Giving**: Build trust through impact reporting
- **Reduced Overhead**: Streamlined donation processing and management
- **Community Building**: Engage with supporters and share your story

## Application Process

### Step 1: Submit Application

1. Visit the organization signup page
2. Provide basic organization information
3. Upload required documentation
4. Submit for review

### Step 2: Verification

Our team reviews applications to ensure:

- Valid non-profit status
- Active operations
- Alignment with Amply's mission
- Compliance with legal requirements

### Step 3: Onboarding

Once approved, you'll receive:

- Access to your organization dashboard
- Training materials and resources
- Dedicated support contact
- Profile setup guidance

### Step 4: Go Live

Complete your profile and start receiving donations:

- Add your organization's story
- Upload photos and videos
- Set up campaigns
- Share with your network

## Timeline

Typical application processing takes 5-10 business days. Complex applications may require additional time.

## Support

Need help? Our organization support team is available to assist with:

- Application questions
- Technical setup
- Best practices
- Troubleshooting
