# Getting Started
*From Application to First Donation*

This guide walks you through joining Amply, from initial application to accepting your first donation.

## Before You Apply

### Check Eligibility

Amply serves verified nonprofits and charitable organizations. Before applying, ensure you:

- Have legal nonprofit or charitable status in your jurisdiction
- Can provide official registration documentation
- Have authorized representatives who can complete identity verification
- Have a bank account in your organization's name

→ [Full requirements](./requirements.md)

### Gather Documentation

You'll need:
- **Registration documents**: Certificate of incorporation, charity registration, or equivalent
- **Tax documentation**: Tax-exempt status confirmation (e.g., 501(c)(3) letter in the US)
- **Bank details**: Account information for receiving payouts
- **Authorized signatory**: Someone who can legally represent the organization

## Application Process

### Step 1: Create Account

Start by creating an organizational account on Amply. You'll need:
- Organization name (as registered)
- Primary contact email
- Country of registration

### Step 2: Submit Verification

Upload your documentation and complete verification:

1. **Legal verification**: Submit registration documents
2. **Identity verification**: Authorized representative confirms identity
3. **Banking verification**: Link your organization's bank account

Verification typically takes 2-5 business days. Complex cases may require additional documentation.

### Step 3: Complete Profile

While verification processes, set up your organization's public profile:

- **Description**: What your organization does
- **Mission statement**: Your purpose and goals
- **SDG alignment**: Which Sustainable Development Goals you address
- **Logo and branding**: Visual identity for your donation pages

→ [SDG framework](../effectiveness/sdg-framework.md)

### Step 4: Configure Donation Settings

Set up how you'll receive donations:

- **Donation amounts**: Suggested giving levels
- **Recurring options**: Monthly, quarterly, annual giving
- **Funds/projects**: How donations should be categorized
- **Thank-you messaging**: Automated donor acknowledgments

### Step 5: Set Up Payouts

Configure how funds reach your bank account:

- **Payout schedule**: Daily, weekly, or monthly
- **Minimum threshold**: Minimum amount before payout triggers
- **Bank verification**: Confirm your banking details

## Going Live

Once verified, you can:

### Launch Donation Pages

Your white-label donation pages are ready to share. Customize the URL, embed on your website, or share directly.

### Invite Team Members

Add staff with appropriate permissions:
- **Admin**: Full access to all settings and data
- **Finance**: Access to transactions and payouts
- **Content**: Edit profile and campaign information
- **View only**: Dashboard access without edit rights

### Connect Integrations

Set up additional channels:
- Website widgets
- Email integration
- CRM connections

→ [Platform tools](./platform-tools.md)

## First Donation

When your first donation arrives:

1. **Immediate visibility**: See it on your dashboard in real-time
2. **Donor information**: Access donor details (if provided)
3. **Automatic receipt**: Donor receives confirmation
4. **Ledger entry**: Transaction recorded with full transparency

Your transparency metrics begin building from day one.

## Next Steps

After going live:

- **Set up reporting**: Configure regular transparency reports
- **Explore analytics**: Understand your donor base
- **Consider fundraisers**: Enable supporters to fundraise on your behalf
- **Track impact**: Connect donations to outcomes

→ [Reporting](./reporting.md)
