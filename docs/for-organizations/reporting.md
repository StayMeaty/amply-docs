# Reporting
*Demonstrate Your Impact with Transparent Data*

Amply provides comprehensive reporting tools to help you understand your fundraising, communicate with stakeholders, and fulfill transparency commitments.

## Transparency Reports

### Automatic Generation

Amply generates transparency reports automatically:

- **Real-time dashboard**: Always current
- **Periodic snapshots**: Weekly, monthly, quarterly, annual
- **Custom date ranges**: Generate reports for any period
- **Public and private versions**: Control what's shared

### Report Contents

Standard transparency reports include:

| Section | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| Income summary | Total donations, sources, trends |
| Spending summary | Expenditure categories, allocations |
| Fund status | Current balances, utilization rates |
| Transparency metrics | Visibility scores, verification status |
| Donor summary | Aggregate donor statistics (not individual data) |

### Customization

Tailor reports to your needs:

- Add narrative sections explaining context
- Include project updates and impact stories
- Attach supporting documentation
- Brand with your organization's identity

## Financial Reports

### Transaction History

Complete record of all financial activity:

- Every incoming donation with details
- Every outgoing expenditure (with visibility controls)
- Payout history
- Fee breakdowns

Export in multiple formats:
- CSV for spreadsheets
- PDF for formal records
- JSON for programmatic access

### Fund Reports

Track designated funds:

- Opening and closing balances
- Inflows and outflows
- Allocation decisions
- Utilization rates

Useful for:
- Restricted fund compliance
- Grant reporting
- Project-specific accounting
- Board presentations

### Tax Documentation

Generate compliant tax receipts for your donors:

- **Tax-deductible receipt generation**
  - Jurisdiction-compliant templates (Australia DGR first)
  - Organization branding and customization
  - Automatic or batch generation
  - Ledger-linked for verification
  - Additional jurisdictions as Amply expands
- Individual donation confirmations (automatic)
- Annual giving summaries
- Batch exports for your records

## Verification Integration

### Cryptographic Verification

All reports are backed by verifiable data:

- Hash chain ensures records can't be altered after recording
- Stripe payment IDs provide independent verification
- Any report can be independently verified
- Public checkpoints confirm consistency over time

→ [Verification guide](../transparency/verification.md)

### Third-Party Audits

Support external audits:

- Export data in auditor-friendly formats
- Provide verification keys
- Document visibility level decisions
- Maintain audit trails

## Report Scheduling

### Automatic Distribution

Set up regular reporting:

- **Recipients**: Who receives each report type
- **Frequency**: Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, annual
- **Format**: PDF, email, dashboard link
- **Triggers**: Threshold-based alerts

### On-Demand Generation

Create reports as needed:

- Custom date ranges
- Specific fund or project focus
- One-time or recurring
- Immediate availability

## Data Export

### Your Data is Yours

Full data portability:

- **Complete export**: All your data in standard formats

- **Donor export**: Supporter data for your systems
- **API access**: Programmatic data retrieval

### Format Options

Export data in:
- CSV (spreadsheet-compatible)
- PDF (formal documents)
- JSON (programmatic access)
