01 Brand Overview

Amply is an ultra-transparent donation platform where every cent is traceable. Our visual identity reflects this commitment to clarity, accessibility, and trust.

Our Mission

We believe charitable giving should be completely transparent. Amply provides the infrastructure for donors to see exactly where their money goes, and for organizations to demonstrate their impact with verifiable data.

Brand Principles

Accessible by Default

Every design choice considers all users. Our typography was selected for optimal readability, and our colors meet WCAG accessibility standards.

Clarity Over Decoration

We favor functional design over ornamental flourishes. Every element serves a purpose. This mirrors our operational philosophy: substance over style.

Trust Through Transparency

Visual honesty matches operational honesty. We don't hide behind complexity or jargon. Our brand is as open as our platform.

Positioning: Guided by ideals. Grounded in reality.

03 Clear Space & Sizing

Clear Space

Maintain adequate space around the wordmark to ensure visibility and impact. The minimum clear space equals the cap height of the letter "A".

Amply
Minimum clear space = 1× cap height of "A"

Minimum Size

To maintain legibility across all applications, observe these minimum sizes:

Amply Large (48pt+)
Amply Medium (24pt)
Amply Minimum (15pt / 20px)
Application Minimum Size Notes
Digital (screen) 20px Favicons may use simplified mark
Print 15pt (~5mm height) Ensure sufficient DPI (300+)
Merchandise 10mm height Production method dependent

Additional Guidelines

Never

  • Place on low-contrast backgrounds
  • Add decorative elements or icons
  • Animate or distort the wordmark
  • Use as part of a sentence
  • Recreate with different typography

Always

  • Use official assets or exact specs
  • Maintain minimum clear space
  • Ensure adequate contrast (4.5:1+)
  • Respect minimum size requirements
  • Keep proportions intact
